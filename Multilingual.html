Multilingualism's benefits

More than one language can enhance children's thinking skills, and also have benefits in better communication abilities and greater cultural understanding.

  • Upgrade memory and brain development
  • Boost problem solving skills
  • Also enhnace cultural awareness
  • Boost pupil's communication confidence

Tips for Multilingual Families

Families creating a rich linguistic environment at home can be helpful for children.

  • Speak your native language daily
  • Read books in distinct languages
  • Use songs, rhymes and games
  • Encourage children to use any language freely
