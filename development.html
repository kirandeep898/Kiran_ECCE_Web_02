Importance of Language Development

  • Builds confidence and self-expression
  • Supports social interaction with others
  • Promoting school readiness and learning
  • Develops thinking and problem-solving skills

    • Role of Adult-Child Interaction

    Language learning relies on positive interactions with adults. Adults can start treating children with respect as conversational partners, listening to them and responding in sensible ways.

    • Respond to your child's voice and words.
    • Ask open-ended questions occasionally.
    • Encourage conversation during everyday activities.
    • Listen carefully and show interest.

    Creating a Language-Rich Home Environment

    creating a language-rich home environment

    Loving communication helps children develop good communication skills. Even everyday tasks can become learning opportunities.

    • Keep books readily available.
    • Verbally describe everyday activities, such as cooking and playing.
    • Reduce screen time and encourage more interaction.
    • Use stories, songs, and games.
